Commodore 64

History

Commodore 64, abbreviated C64, was introduced in 1982 and gained worldwide popularity. It is still the most sold computer in the world.

Current status

There's still a wide community of C64 enthusiasts. There are numerous C64 emulators, such as CCS64 (link to http://www.ccs64.com/) and Frodo (link to http://frodo.cebix.net/).

Gaming device

Best games Commodore 64 was notorious for its magnificent games. Some of the best include:

  • contained 60 levels
  • originally a ZX Spectrum game
  • Last Ninja 2
  • Ultima 5

Joysticks
